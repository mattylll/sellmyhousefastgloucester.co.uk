Our Story

Sell My House Fast Gloucester was founded to solve a problem that too many homeowners in this historic city face: the slow, uncertain, and often stressful process of selling a property through traditional channels. Gloucester is a city of remarkable depth, from its stunning Norman cathedral and regenerated Victorian docks to the terraced streets of Kingsholm and the suburban estates that expanded the city in the twentieth century. Its property market reflects that variety, and so do the challenges sellers encounter.

Our founders built this business from direct experience of Gloucester's property market. They saw homeowners in Longlevens waiting months for buyers who never materialised. They watched families in Barnwood trapped in chains that stretched across Gloucestershire and collapsed at the last minute. They met retirees in Tuffley who could not downsize because their properties needed work that open-market buyers were unwilling to take on.

The solution was straightforward: create a service that gives Gloucester homeowners a genuine alternative. From our first purchase, a Victorian terrace in Kingsholm that the owner needed to sell within a fortnight due to a job relocation, our approach has remained unchanged. We make a fair cash offer, handle all the legal work, and complete on a date that suits the seller, not one dictated by mortgage lenders and surveyors.

Since those early days, we have purchased hundreds of properties across every part of Gloucester. We have helped homeowners facing repossession in Matson, supported landlords in Kingsholm exiting the rental market, and worked with families going through divorce who needed a clean financial break. Every transaction has reinforced our commitment to making property sales as stress-free as possible. Today, Sell My House Fast Gloucester is one of the city's most trusted cash buying services, but we remain a local company at heart, with a team that knows every neighbourhood from Quedgeley to Longlevens, and a reputation built on delivering exactly what we promise.

Local Expertise in Gloucester

Gloucester's property market is shaped by factors that are unique to this Severn-side city, and understanding them is essential to making fair, accurate offers. The city's position at the head of the Severn Estuary, its industrial heritage, the ongoing docks regeneration, and the influence of the rugby community in Kingsholm all create distinct property micro-markets that require genuine local knowledge to value properly.

Our expertise covers every corner of Gloucester. In Kingsholm, we understand the mix of Victorian and Edwardian terraces that surround the rugby ground, the premium that period features and proximity to the city centre command, and the particular maintenance challenges of the older housing stock. In Longlevens, we know the wide range of inter-war and post-war family housing that makes the area one of Gloucester's most popular residential neighbourhoods.

In Quedgeley, we have tracked the expansion of new-build estates alongside the older village core, understanding how new developments affect values across the wider area. In Barnwood, we appreciate the mix of housing types from Victorian cottages to modern estates, and the area's convenient position between the city centre and the M5 motorway.

Tuffley, Hucclecote, and Matson each bring different market characteristics. From Tuffley's post-war housing estates to Hucclecote's family-friendly suburban character and Matson's affordable homes on the city's southern edge, we understand the local factors that influence value. When we make an offer on your Gloucester property, it is based on genuine expertise, not guesswork or national averages.

Why Choose Cash Buyers in Gloucester

Selling a property in Gloucester through a traditional estate agent can be a lengthy and uncertain process. The city's affordable price point, while positive for buyers, means that the market is heavily dependent on mortgage availability. When lending conditions tighten, demand drops quickly, and sellers can find themselves waiting months for buyers who qualify. The average time from listing to completion in Gloucester typically runs to four to six months, and that assumes everything goes smoothly.

When you sell to Sell My House Fast Gloucester, you bypass all of these risks. As cash house buyers, we do not rely on mortgage approvals, so there is no lender to delay or decline the purchase. We do not sit in a chain, so your sale cannot collapse because of problems elsewhere. And because we buy properties in any condition, you do not need to invest in the repairs and renovations that Gloucester's older housing stock sometimes demands.

Estate agents in Gloucester typically charge between one and two per cent commission, plus VAT. On a property at the city's average price of £240,000, that could mean fees of £2,880 to £5,760 before you account for legal costs, EPC fees, and months of mortgage payments while you wait for a buyer. With us, there are no fees, no commission, and no hidden charges. We cover your basic legal costs, and the price we agree is the price you receive.

Speed is often what matters most. Whether you need to sell because of a relationship breakdown, a career move away from Gloucestershire, financial pressures, or an inherited property that needs settling, our ability to complete in as little as seven days gives you control over a process that traditionally leaves sellers waiting. From Kingsholm to Quedgeley, from Longlevens to Matson, we help Gloucester homeowners move forward on their own terms.
