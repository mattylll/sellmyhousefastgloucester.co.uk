Serving notice and waiting for vacant possession extends the timeline by months, and if tenants do not leave voluntarily, the court process adds further delay.</p><h3>Property Condition</h3><p>Rental properties in Gloucester often show wear from tenanted use. Carpets, decorating, kitchens, and bathrooms may be functional but tired. The property may not present well for viewings compared to owner-occupied homes. This condition gap can suppress the achievable price.</p><h3>Tax on the Sale</h3><p>Capital Gains Tax applies to the profit on the sale. For landlords who bought in Gloucester ten or fifteen years ago, when prices were significantly lower, the gain can be substantial. CGT rates on property are 18% for basic rate taxpayers and 24% for higher rate taxpayers. Agent fees of 1-2% plus VAT apply.</p><h3>Sell Tenanted via Estate Agent</h3><p>Marketing a tenanted property through a Gloucester agent limits the buyer pool to investors. Expect a 10-15% discount below vacant-possession value, a longer marketing period, and the same agent fees.</p><h3>Sell to a Cash Buyer</h3><p>We can purchase your Gloucester rental property with tenants in place, in any condition, and complete in as little as two weeks.
