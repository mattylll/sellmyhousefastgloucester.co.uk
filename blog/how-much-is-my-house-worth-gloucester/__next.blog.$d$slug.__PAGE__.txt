Well-maintained, south-facing gardens add measurable value.</p><h3>Parking</h3><p>Off-street parking is increasingly important. Getting three agent valuations and looking for consensus is sensible practice.</p><h3>RICS Surveys</h3><p>For the most authoritative valuation, a RICS-qualified surveyor provides an independent, professional assessment. This costs between £300 and £600 but provides a figure that mortgage lenders and solicitors recognise as credible. For non-standard construction properties, a RICS survey can be particularly valuable in establishing a defensible valuation.</p><h3>Cash Buyer Valuations</h3><p>Cash buyers like us provide free, no-obligation valuations based on comparable data and direct experience of buying across Gloucester.
